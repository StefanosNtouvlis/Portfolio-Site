Information about me

Hi, I'm Stefanos Ntouvlis, a professional with a strong background in Informatics , Telecommunications , Information Systems & Project Management. I recently completed my master's degree in Information Studies at the University of Amsterdam, where I gained experience in data analysis and integrating technology into business strategies. My thesis focused on using technology to enhance social connectedness through a quiz game, providing insights into how tech can improve daily life. I have also worked extensively in Networking and Wireless Technologies, developing leadership and management skills through my studies at UoP, and professional experiences at CHS Harvard and AKETH. I'm passionate about leveraging my technical expertise and strategic thinking to drive results and make a meaningful impact within a dynamic team.
